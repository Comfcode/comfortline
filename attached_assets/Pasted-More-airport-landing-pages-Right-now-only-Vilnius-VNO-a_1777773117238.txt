Internal linking audit. Add a "Related routes" block on every page, breadcrumbs everywhere, contextual cross-links in body copy. Currently most pages are SEO islands.

Performance (Core Web Vitals). Audit LCP / CLS / INP — both Google and Yandex (ICS score) rank by speed. Likely wins: lazy-load offscreen images, preload the hero font and image, defer non-critical JS, drop unused Radix components.
