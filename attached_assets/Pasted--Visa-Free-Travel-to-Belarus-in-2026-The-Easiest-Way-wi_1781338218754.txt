# Visa-Free Travel to Belarus in 2026 – The Easiest Way with a Private Transfer

## Visit Belarus Without a Visa

Belarus currently offers a visa-free regime for citizens of many European countries, making it easier than ever to discover Minsk, Mir Castle, Nesvizh Castle, Brest Fortress, Belovezhskaya Pushcha and other unique destinations.

If you are a citizen of Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Sweden, the United Kingdom and many other European countries, you may be eligible to enter Belarus without obtaining a visa in advance.

Official rules and eligibility:
https://mfa.gov.by/en/visa/freemove/europe/
